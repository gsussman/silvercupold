Investment Philosophy

Silver Cup Capital’s investment philosophy is to deploy capital in situations where we believe potential reward outweighs investment risk so as to achieve consistent, risk-adjusted returns for our investors. We seek to do this by properly aligning people, capital, knowledge, incentives and support systems. This proper alignment starts with identifying regions and markets we believe have high potential for our investors and identifying and partnering with proven high-quality operating partners in each region and market in which we operate. Each of these two building blocks is further enhanced by Silver Cup Capital processes.

Our geographic focus is enhanced by our cluster approach, which seeks to reduce relevant investment risks through diversification within and among various geographic regions and the work of our operating partners is enhanced by building out incentive structures and support systems to align the interests of all parties with those of our investors.

Silver Cup Capital’s investment activities operate according to the principles of our investment philosophy:

Belief in Market Fundamentals

Silver Cup Capital believes that there is no substitution for hard numbers. We review population, crime, employment, economic, education, and other relevant demographic and investment data to identify the regions, markets, areas, and property types that we believe have potential for growth. Additionally, rather than purchasing speculatively, Silver Cup Capital works backwards, anticipating cashflows and limiting costs to those justifiable for such anticipated cashflows.

Dedication to Process

Silver Cup Capital believes that each investment must be subjected to rigorous and systematic evaluation and seeks to apply such framework for every investment. Once invested, Silver Cup Capital uses established management processes to enhance both property cashflows and maintain properties to position them for resale.

Flexibility and Diversity with Respect to Geography and Asset Types

Silver Cup Capital believes that a disciplined evaluation process and the application of fundamental analysis can be applied across asset types and geographies. For this reason, Silver Cup Capital attempts to deploy capital opportunistically across asset types, including single family residences, small multi-family residences, small- and mid-sized apartment complexes and across our target geographies, in accordance with the cluster approach and investor appetite.
